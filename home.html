Welcome to my Portfolio!

From a young age, I never seem to understand why I heard so much noise from my big brother’s room. His friends would come over and would start shouting at the television as figures of people ran across the screen.

Soon enough, my brother would figure out how little Charlotte was peeking through the door to see what he was doing. This is where I would find my passion for the sports industry. My brother started to include me in watching games.

Watching “Friday Night Knicks” will always be one of my favorite memories. Fast forward, I attended Michigan State University and become a D-1 Basketball Reporter covering Tom Izzo and Suzy Merchant’s basketball teams. I wanted to be closer to home, and transferred to my forever dream school Syracuse University and attended the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications.

I work now with Syracuse Athletics as a social media and creative media intern for a variety of sports such as basketball and lacrosse and have become a sports intern for the New York Post. I might be living the dream that my brother always wished for but I am striving to show that woman can work in sports and not be told go work in the kitchen and do the dishes.
