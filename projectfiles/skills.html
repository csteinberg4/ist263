My Work!

Ever since I can remember I have always had a knack to talk basketball. I become very active on Twitter in 2020, and my profile blew up. I founded the first-ever female led Knicks fanpage @LetsTalkKnicks now currently with over 11,000 followers to date. As I grew the following with commentary each game, news outlets started to reach out to feature me. Since 2021, I have spoke with the New York Post as shown above as well various other New York outlets.

For the New York Post, I appear sometimes on television to talk the morning after a New York Knicks game or to give an analysis on a trade or acquisition the Knicks have made. I am extremely grateful to be able to say that the connections I have made from these two organizations have led me to internships, connections, and real-world experience.
